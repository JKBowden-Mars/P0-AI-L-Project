# Examples of hard to understand operational request the AI system has been calibrated to interpret and decifer.

## Example 1

**“Hey, not sure who owns this but the dashboard hasn’t updated since yesterday and leadership is asking about it.”**

#### Why it’s messy:

* No owner

- Implied urgency

- External pressure

- No clear action

## Example 2

**"I am not sure why, but my inbox was just flooded with urgent looking docs, I doubt they are for me but is anyone looking for these they might be urgent.**

#### Why it's messy:

- No owner

- Frantic urgency

- No clear action

## Example 3

**"Sup, a teams files endedup in my directory that could be from my team but im working on that thing for leadership so I don't think I can check. I don't think they are urgent but they might be!"**

- Rambling

- Contradictory Urgency

- Contradictory Ownership

## Example 4

**"Hey did anyone ever figure out how to fix that copying problem we were having in the one team's directory? How do I find my and their files too I been struggling with that too. But please dont forget the copying"**

- Emotional language

- Unclear Ownership

- Multiple problems/Rambling

## Example 5

**"How do I get into that folder leadership told you all to give me access to yesterday? The clients profile folder looks outdated too. Someone needs to come give me access right now!"**

- External Pressure

- Multiple Issues
